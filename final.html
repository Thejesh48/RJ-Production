Bringing People, Moments, and Sound Together

RJ Production is a Bangalore-based event media and sound company, working quietly behind the scenes to help events unfold smoothly and leave a lasting impression. The company is led by Jyothi Hegde, whose approach emphasizes attentiveness, preparation, and respect for both the event and the people behind it. This leadership philosophy reflects in how we work—calmly, thoughtfully, and with care.

Photography

We photograph events with attentiveness and respect for the moment. Our focus stays on people—expressions, interactions, and the atmosphere that defines the day. From formal corporate gatherings to carefully planned ceremonies, we document events in a way that feels composed yet genuine.

Sound

Sound shapes how an event is experienced. We approach audio with care—ensuring speeches are heard comfortably, discussions remain clear, and music supports the moment without overpowering it. Every setup is planned, tested, and monitored so that sound remains reliable throughout.
